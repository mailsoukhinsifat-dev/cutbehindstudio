Questions
FAQ

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

Book a Call →
How fast is the turnaround?
Most projects are delivered within 48 hours of receiving all raw files. For complex long-form edits, we agree on a deadline upfront — always within 5 business days.
How many revisions do I get?
Unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. We don't believe in capping rounds — our job is to deliver exactly what you envision.
What editing software do you use?
We work primarily in Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve for color grading, with After Effects for motion graphics. We can also work in Final Cut Pro on request.
Do you offer monthly retainer packages?
Yes — our most popular option is a monthly retainer where we handle a fixed number of videos per month at a discounted rate. We discuss this on the strategy call.
How do I send raw footage?
Via Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or Frame.io — whichever works best for you. We send a shared folder link immediately after onboarding.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a first video at no cost for qualified creators and brands — up to 5 minutes of raw footage. Book the strategy call to see if you qualify. Only 3 spots are open per month.
